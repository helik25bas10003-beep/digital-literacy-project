# Social Media Checklist for College Students

## Do's
- Maintain a professional and respectful profile  
- Share useful and meaningful content  
- Verify information before posting or sharing  
- Use privacy settings to control your data  
- Think before you post anything online  

## Don'ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly  
- Avoid posting inappropriate or offensive content  
- Do not engage in online arguments or negativity  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown sources  
- Do not spread misinformation or fake news
