# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible  
- Do not click on suspicious links or unknown attachments  
- Verify the authenticity of emails, messages, and websites before responding  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like logging into accounts  
- Keep your device software and apps updated regularly  

## Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone  
- Do not accept unknown payment requests or scan random QR codes  
- Always verify the receiver’s details before sending money  
- Use only official banking or UPI apps for transactions  

## Reporting Cybercrime
- Report incidents at: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930 (24x7 support for financial fraud)
